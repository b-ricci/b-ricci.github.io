How to Embed a YouTube Video on Your Vimeo Website

This tutorial explains how to embed a YouTube video into your Vimeo website

Step 1: Choose a Video

  1. Go to YouTube
  2. Find a video
  3. Click the video you want to embed

Step 2: Click the Share Button

  1. Under the video, click the Share button

Step 3: Copy the Embed Code

  1. Click Embed
  2. Copy the iframe code

Step 4: Find the video you would like to display on your Vimeo webpage

  1. Click the share button
  2. Add the desired size of your video
  3. Adjust the settings to your preference
  4. Your changes will be displayed on the video preview

Step 5: Place your video on your webpage

  1. Copy and paste the embed code to be placed on your webpage
